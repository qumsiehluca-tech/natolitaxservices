Sales Agent Overview

We're looking for motivated individuals to help spread the word about Natoli Tax Services. There are no restrictions on how you find clients — use any method you prefer.

All potential clients must complete our 5-question eligibility screener. If they qualify, you have two options:

Option 1

Website Referral — 10% Commission

  1. Direct the client to our website.
  2. Have them enter your name as the referral agent.
  3. Once the client completes the process and payment is received, you earn 10%.

Option 2

Full-Service Referral — 20% Commission

  1. Administer the eligibility screener yourself.
  2. If the client qualifies, collect all required tax information and documents.
  3. Submit the completed information to us.
  4. Once the client's payment is received, you earn 20%.
