Job Description

There are no restrictions on how you find clients, use any method you prefer. All potential clients must complete our 5-question eligibility screener.

If they pass, you have two options:

Option 1: Website Referral (10% Commission)

  1. Direct the client to our website.
  2. Have them enter your name as the referral agent.
  3. Once the client completes the process and payment is received, you will earn a 10% commission.

Option 2: Full-Service Referral (20% Commission)

  1. Administer the eligibility screener yourself.
  2. If the client qualifies, collect all required tax information and documents.
  3. Submit the completed information to us.
  4. Once the client's payment is received, you will earn a 20% commission.

(Additional detailed instructions will be provided upon acceptance.)

Important Notes

  • This is a commission-only position (no base pay).
  • You will work as an independent contractor (1099).
